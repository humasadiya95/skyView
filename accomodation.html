Skyview Gondola View Cottages

Experience hospitality as exquisite as Jammu and Kashmir in our dome-shaped Skyview Gondola View Cottages inspired by the natural environment of the region and that offers the opportunity to be engulfed by the picturesque setting of Sanget Valley.

Skyview Mountain View Rooms

Doing Justice to the Tranquil Setting of Skyview by Empyrean

Skyview Mountain View Rooms offer a glimpse of downtown Kashmir with its traditional interiors. The rooms allow you to absorb the magnificent view of the Shivalik mountains over the lush green forest in summers or for you to enjoy the spectacle of snow-covered mountains in the background.

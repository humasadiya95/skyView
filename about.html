About Skyview

A Frontrunner In Adventure Tourism

Skyview by Empyrean is a frontrunner in adventure tourism, leisure, lifestyle activities, hospitality, blended with artistic luxury.

It focuses on mountain tourism and seeks to introduce the thrill of being outdoors while abiding holistically to responsible and sustainable tourism. It is determined in promoting green urban mobility by collaborating with leading global brands and partnering with the best national and international names. It celebrates the oneness of the human spirit with nature in its abundance.

Pertinent to mention that Skyview by Empyrean takes immense pride in being a signatory to Future of Tourism, a member/affiliate of the following tourism associations and bodies: ATOAI, TAAI, ADTOI, IATO, and J&K Tourism. Additionally, it is an Associate Member of World Travel and Tourism Council (WTTC), Grand Patron of Responsible Tourism Society of India (RTSOI), a Signatory Member of World Travel Mart (WTM), member of “Leave no trace” and of the Indo French Chamber of Commerce and Industry (IFCCI).

Its first property is located at the foothills of the Shivalik Range of the Himalayas and the Pir Panjal mountains in Sanget-Patnitop, Jammu and Kashmir.

It was created in partnership with prominent global companies including POMA Group, MND Group, ERIC, and Sage Geotech in the planning, development and construction of Skyview by Empyrean.

Skyview by Empyrean is an initiative by Empyrean Skyview Projects Pvt. Ltd. [ESPPL] (a wholly-owned subsidiary of FIL Industries Private Limited).

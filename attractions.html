Nearby Attractions

Skyview by Empyrean is a destination like no other that offers the best of outdoor activities, hospitality, dining, luxury accommodation and much more at the foothills of the Himalayas in Jammu and Kashmir.

Patnitop

One of the most scenic hill stations of India, Patnitop features rolling meadows, evergreen forests and picturesque sights making it one of the best places to visit near vaishno devi.

Things to do:

Explore the 600 year old Naag Mandir, go for treks and leisurely walks, enjoy cycling, and other recreational activities.

Best time to visit:

Patnitop is a year round destination. The summers are pleasant and cool, and in winter witness snowfall.

Madhtop

A 5 km trek from Patnitop, Madhatop is a nature loverâ€™s paradise and is probably one of the best tourist places in Jammu. From fresh water springs, dense virgin woods, to scenic views of the mountains, Madhatop has it all.

Things to do:

Trekking and paragliding in the summer.

Best time to visit:

Most of the year when you can enjoy treks and scenic views.

Nathatop

Just a 30 min drive from Patnitop, witness the panoramic 180 degree views of the Pir Panjal Range of the Himalayas. Soak in the scent of the pine forests. Stop a local tea shop for Chai and the Kaladi, a local cheese.

Things to do:

Hike to the topmost point for mesmerizing view of the Himalayas, enjoy paragliding and walk about in the open terrain.

Best time to visit:

Most the year, however during heavy snow, the access is limited.

Sanasar

Named after two small lakes Sana and Sar, Sanasar is only 60 minutes away from Patnitop. A perfect picnic destination thanks to its undulating landscape. The drive towards Sanasar, is scenic and memorable allowing you to soak in the natural beauty and make pit stops for photographs amidst picturesque settings. Once you are in Sanasar, you can enjoy pony rides, adventure activities, or just spend time lazing on the meadows. If you are looking for best tourist places in Jammu then you should definitely add Sanasar in your checklist.

Things to do:

Paragliding, Wilderness Adventure Camps (WAC), trekking, 9-hole golf course, shikara ride on the lake and visit the 400-year old Shank Pal temple.

Best time to visit:

April to October.
